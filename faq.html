What is this?

Cut Up is a text randomizer. You can use it to find fresh ideas by randomizing old lyrics you have, or lyrics of songs you like. Or any text at all really.

How do I use it?

Paste text into the box and click randomize. Play around with the settings for interesting results!

What is the "cut up method"?

A method for writing lyrics popularized by David Bowie (or William Burroughs, I'm not sure). You can read more about it in this article.

Why so plain?

I think it looks and performs better this way.
